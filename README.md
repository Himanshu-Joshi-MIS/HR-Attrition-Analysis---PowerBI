# HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze employee attrition and identify key factors affecting workforce retention.

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- Data Cleaning
- DAX
- Data Modeling

## 📊 Key Metrics
- Total Employees: 1470  
- Total Attrition: 237  
- Attrition Rate: 16.1%  
- Average Age: 37  
- Average Salary: 6.5K  

## 📈 Analysis Performed
- Attrition by Gender  
- Attrition by Education  
- Attrition by Salary  
- Attrition by Job Role  
- Attrition by Age Group  
- Attrition by Years at Company  

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Employees in the 26–35 age group have the highest attrition  
- Maximum attrition occurs in salary band below 5K  
- Laboratory Technician & Sales Executive roles show high turnover  
- Male attrition is slightly higher than female  
- Life Sciences education background has highest attrition  



## 🚀 Conclusion
This dashboard helps HR teams identify patterns in employee attrition and supports data-driven decision-making to improve retention.
